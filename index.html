    Mesonet Explorer

    Weather & soil data from the Montana Mesonet

    Time modes

    • Latest — most recent observation at each station, auto-refreshed. Stations reporting more than 3 hours ago are hidden as stale (toggle them back in the legend).
    • Hourly — pick any date and hour to see hourly values across the network.
    • Daily — pick any date to see daily aggregates (totals for precipitation, means for most other variables).

    How to use it

    • Variable — choose from air, wind, precipitation, soil, ET, and groundwater measurements. The list adapts to the time mode: precipitation accumulations and soil-moisture change appear in Latest, reference ET in Hourly/Daily, and growing degree days in Daily.
    • Search — press / to jump there; ↑/↓ + Enter to pick a station. (Add ?kbd=off to the URL to disable the / shortcut.)
    • Networks — chips toggle HydroMet / AgriMet stations.
    • Hover a dot for the station's value; click for details, a photo, and a dashboard link.
    • Units — °F/°C toggles between U.S. and metric units.
    • Legend — click the No-data / Stale rows to show or hide those stations; check boxes to add counties, watersheds, or live radar.
    • Export — download a branded PNG of the current view (fixed Montana framing, color scale included).
    • Esc closes any popup.

    Share or bookmark

    The URL reflects every choice — use the share button or copy it from the address bar. ?station=<id> deep-links a single station.

    Data

    Data come live from the Montana Mesonet API v2. Radar imagery courtesy of the Iowa Environmental Mesonet. The Montana Mesonet is operated by the Montana Climate Office at the University of Montana.
